About Antiwork

r/Antiwork is a subreddit devoted to discussing modern work culture, as well as advocating for work reform and unionization. Founded in 2013, it saw a sharp increase in activity during the Covid-19 pandemic as work life rapidly evolved due to social isolation. Normalization of work from home led to a widespread reexamining of the ideal work-life balance and what's one's labor is truly worth.

This project explores the popularity of the antiwork movement, both within its dedicated subreddit and in the larger Twittersphere.
